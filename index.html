Venue

All of our guests are welcome and encouraged to stay at Berkshire Lake Camp for our wedding weekend. We will add pictures in the summer, but if you would like an impression now, the venue's website is here

The cabins are rustic, but have electricity, wifi and bathrooms for each room. Each room contains 7 (single) bunkbeds, but they do not have to be filled. We have enough cabins that no one has to share a bunkbed if they do not want to.

The cost to stay at Berkshire Lake Camp is $104 per person for the whole weekend (Children under 5 are free). This fee covers your accomodations. Unfortunately, you cannot pay to the campsite directly. When we receive your RSVP, we will send you payment instructions.

If our cabins are outside your or your body's comfort zone, we understand that you may want to make alternative sleeping arrangements. There are hotels and (air)b&bs within a reasonable driving distance from the lake, but be aware that they can get quite expensive if not booked early. Whether or not you are staying with us at the lake, everyone is invited to all meals and activities.

How do I get there?

The best international airports are Boston Logan International Airport (BOS), New York’s JFK International Airport (JFK), and Newark Liberty International Airport (EWR). The closest airport is in Albany, NY, which is connected to most major US cities east of the Mississippi.

Pittsfield, MA is a 20 min drive from our campsite and has good connections to the major cities in the North East. Let us know if you want us to pick you up from there. If your particular journey wants you to arrive extra early on Friday, that should be more than fine but please let us know.

Pittsfield station is located on the Amtrak “Lake Shore Limited” line between Boston and Albany (and eventually Chicago), which carries one train daily in each direction (1PM from Boston, 3:30PM from Albany (though the latter train is often delayed)). Guests from New Haven and New York City could consider taking the Vermonter line to Springfield, MA and transfer to the Lake Shore Limited line there.
